QUESTIONS

Frequently asked

What is Stamped?
Stamped is an AI job documentation platform built for Singapore managing agents. When a contractor completes a job, they send a voice note via WhatsApp. Stamped automatically transcribes it, attaches before/after photos, timestamps the record, and creates a permanent, structured job card in the managing agent's dashboard — in under 60 seconds.
How long does it take a contractor to log a job?
Under 60 seconds. Contractors use a 4-screen mobile app accessed via a WhatsApp invite link — no separate app download, no login to remember. Most contractors are logging their first job in under 10 minutes from invite.
Is Stamped PDPA compliant?
Yes. Stamped stores data on Singapore-region servers with role-restricted access, in full compliance with Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act. Full data processing documentation is available on request.
How does the AI job card work?
When a contractor submits a job, Stamped transcribes the voice note, classifies the job type, extracts key details (location, scope, materials), and structures everything into a standardised job card with geotagged before/after photos and a timestamp — formatted consistently regardless of which contractor submits.
Can I use IMDA SME Go Digital to fund this?
Stamped is being registered under the IMDA SME Go Digital programme. Once listed, eligible Singapore-registered companies can claim up to 70% in grant support. At the Growth tier of SGD 249/month, this reduces the effective cost to around SGD 75/month. Contact us for the current grant status.
