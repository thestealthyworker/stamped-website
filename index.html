Questions

Frequently asked

What is Stamped?
Stamped is an AI job documentation platform built for Singapore managing agents. When a contractor completes a job, they send a voice note via WhatsApp. Stamped automatically transcribes it, attaches before/after photos, timestamps the record, and creates a permanent, structured job card in the managing agent's dashboard — in under 60 seconds.
How long does it take a contractor to log a job?
Under 60 seconds. Contractors use a 4-screen mobile app accessed via a WhatsApp invite link — no separate app download, no login to remember. They describe the job in a voice note, attach photos, and tap to submit. Most contractors are logging their first job in under 10 minutes from invite.
Is Stamped PDPA compliant?
Yes. Stamped is built with Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act requirements in mind. Data is stored on Singapore-region servers, access is role-restricted, and all photo and personal data handling follows PDPA obligations. Full data processing documentation is available on request.
How does the AI job card work?
When a contractor submits a job, Stamped uses AI to transcribe the voice note, classify the job type, extract key details (location, scope, materials), and structure everything into a standardised job card. The card includes geotagged before/after photos, a timestamp, and contractor details — formatted consistently regardless of which contractor submits.
Can I use IMDA SME Go Digital to fund this?
Stamped is being registered under the IMDA SME Go Digital programme. Once listed, eligible Singapore-registered companies can claim up to 70% in grant support. At the Growth tier of SGD 249/month, this reduces the effective cost to around SGD 75/month. Contact us for the current grant status.
